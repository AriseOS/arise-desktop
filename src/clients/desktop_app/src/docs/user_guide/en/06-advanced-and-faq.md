## 7. Advanced Features and FAQ

### 7.1 Conversational Generation

In **Conversational Generation** you can describe what you want in natural language:

- Type a goal such as "Monitor new orders in my dashboard and export them".
- The app attempts to generate an appropriate workflow.

This is an alternative to recording-based generation and is best for experienced users.

### 7.2 Advanced Workflow Editing

Some versions of Ami expose advanced workflow editors where you can:

- Inspect flows as graphs.
- Tweak conditions and branching.

### 7.3 FAQ and Troubleshooting

Common issues include:

- **Environment checks failing**: verify that the browser and backend service are available.
- **Login problems**: confirm your credentials and network connectivity.
- **Workflows not running as expected**: open the execution result view and check error messages step by step.

As the product evolves you can expand this section with more targeted questions and answers.
